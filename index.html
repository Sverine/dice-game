Rules

Welcome to the dice game

This game contains two players on the same screen.
Every single player has : a temporary score and a global score.

At each turn, the temporary score of player is initialized to 0 and the current player can roll the dice as many times as he want.
The result of the throw is added to the temporary score.

During the player's turn, the player can decide to :

  • Save his temporary score by clicking on Hold button : this action will automatically passed to the other player's turn.
  • Keep rolling dice : if the number of dice is 1, the temporary score is initialized to 0 and the player skip his turn.

The first player who reach 100 points win the game

Good luck !
